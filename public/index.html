Eunice Kim

Eunice is a collaborative interaction / UX designer for a variety of responsive, modular interfaces and alternative imaging devices. She has a background in multimedia arts and technology to explore the imaginative use of communications technologies - specialize in ideas and solutions to existing problem, improve and bring delight and art into people's lives while balancing creativity with functionality.

Ms. Kim has a Master’s degree in the Interactive Telecommunication Program from New York University’s Tisch School of Arts and a BFA in Multimedia Arts from School of Visual Arts. She has been transforming the space at the American Museum of Natural History for #OneStepBeyond since 2011. Eunice is a collaborative interactive and UI/UX designer for a variety of responsive, modular interfaces and alternative imaging devices. These responsive visual performance systems empower users to play and produce media together in real time.
