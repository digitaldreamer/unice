My Role: Project Manager & UX Designer
I carefully managed deliverables and timeline for the product.
As the UX Designer I conducted case studies of the hardware capabilities and API, user analysis, personas and created user friendly prototypes.

Summary: Mama Knows Best’s app is a free dieting tool that allows users to track their food and exercise through Jawbone, and stay motivated to make smarter choices and achieve their diet.

OBJECTIVE
This project was presented at Codethon 2014 for the Women’s Health Event. (sponsored by Jawbone and Self Magazine)
Create an extension of the existing Women’s Health app for Jawbone that integrate a system that contacts responder in health & nutrition.

Concept

Sometimes expert opinions from scientists, doctors, and psychologists can be impersonal and come across as harsh which might make people feel bad. Our mothers are comforting and nurturing and give the best loving advice.

Goal

Our goal is to help women create a better diet by using Jawbone and our app.

Comparative Analysis for Diet App

What feature that exist within the Mama Knows the Best app?

  • No calories number, Auto-calculate and suggestions the low calories base on food history.
  • Detail comparison between food calories and exercise calories you burn a workout.
  • Speaking and listening to interaction approach to staying motivated to make smarter choices with love and happiness and no more stress in the relationship with your body.

User Research

What is the reason that women tried to lose weight and failed?

  • Reason #1: Underestimating calories is not an issue when you don’t have to count them at all.
  • Reason #2: For weight loss, diet counts more than exercise. If you are not counting.calories for food, then do not sweat over how many calories you burn during a workout.
  • Reason #3: If you don’t want to eat, then don’t. But don’t binge either.
Persona

Katherine, Age 34: Female lives in Astoria, who are on special dietary need.

Katherine is a product manager at a digital agency.
She has light diabetes so healthy eating is very important for health. Although she tries to keep her goal, it is not easy during the weekdays. She really needs help with her diet which cause so much stress.
